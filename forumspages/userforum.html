Clean Nissan R34 Skyline GT-R

The Nissan R34 Skyline, introduced in 1998, is the fourth generation of the iconic Skyline sports car series. Renowned for its striking design and high-performance capabilities, the R34 continued the Skyline's legacy of excellence. Available in various trims, including the GT-R, GT-T, and GT-X, the R34 featured a range of engine options, with the RB26DETT twin-turbocharged inline-six engine powering the GT-R variant. The R34 Skyline showcased advancements in aerodynamics and technology, with features such as the ATTESA E-TS Pro all-wheel-drive system and the Super-HICAS four-wheel steering system. Celebrated for its success in motorsports, including dominating performances in JGTC racing, the R34 Skyline holds a special place in automotive history as a symbol of Japanese sports car ingenuity and performance excellence.
