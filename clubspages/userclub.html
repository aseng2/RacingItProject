Brekkie Car Club

In 2020, amidst the COVID-19 pandemic, with businesses closing and the coffee industry struggling, a small group of car enthusiasts (us!) founded Brekkie Car Club (BCC) to get people out of the house and support small businesses in our local communities.

We organized Sunday morning meets, bridging our love for cars and coffee to drive traffic to these businesses and offer a chance for people to make new friends.

Over time, BCC grew into Southern California's largest car meet. With love and support from our community, we have been consistently hosting events with over 400 vehicles in attendance, connecting people with our favorite friends and businesses.
