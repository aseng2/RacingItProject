Chuckwalla Valley Raceway

Chuckwalla Valley Raceway (CVR) stands as a premier grand prix circuit with a strong emphasis on ensuring safety and delivering an exhilarating experience for both automobiles and motorcycles. Spanning a distance of 2.68 miles and stretching 40 feet in width, the track boasts a dynamic configuration comprising 17 smoothly interconnected bends. Its undulating terrain presents an array of elevation shifts that pose a formidable test even for the most seasoned drivers and riders. Particularly renowned for its iconic "The Bowl" section, Chuckwalla holds a special place in the hearts of enthusiasts due to its diverse range of corners, demanding a high level of skill to master this intricate and demanding course.
