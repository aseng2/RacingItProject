Thunderhill Raceway Park

With a 2 mile, 3 mile and 5 mile track as well as 2 skid pads, Thunderhill is the raceway of choice for serious racers, clubs and those looking to push their vehicle to the limit.

2 Mile Thunderhill West: A more technical track which rewards precision. The variety of corners, including linked corners, makes it a great learning environment.

3 Mile East Track: With 15 turns and a combination of long, sweeping turns and tighter, complex sections, the 3 mile circuit is ideal for racing and testing your driving skills.

5 Mile Full Circuit: Experience the full thrill of Thunderhill with our five-mile full circuit. More turns, more straights, more action and more chance to push your vehicle to the edge.
